Inspiration

“I set out to help diagnose and help users keep track of there usage habits.

But after speaking to real people struggling with addiction I realized they were spending thousands on therapists, online programs, and medications.

The mental health space is constantly growing but finding solutions are restricted to word-of-mouth, companies who spend millions on advertising, or the 1st page of Google.

Ceritas is redesigning how we search for mental health support. We’re helping people find the best resources that fit their needs. And encouraging new growth and ideas in the mental health sphere.”

Co-Founder, Dylan Tan
